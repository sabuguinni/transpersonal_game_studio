# Underwater Systems Concept - Marine Biome Diversity

## Visual Concept Description
Comprehensive underwater ecosystem design for the Cretaceous marine environment:

### Underwater Terrain Features
- **Abyssal Plains**: Vast flat seabed areas at maximum depth (-200m to -500m) with sparse but unique life
- **Underwater Canyons**: Deep trenches and gorges creating dramatic topography and shelter for large predators
- **Seamounts**: Underwater mountains rising from the depths, creating unique ecosystems and navigation landmarks
- **Coral Formations**: Prehistoric reef systems supporting diverse marine communities
- **Hydrothermal Vents**: Active geological features providing heat, minerals, and specialized ecosystems

### Marine Life Stratification
- **Surface Layer (0-50m)**: Flying reptiles diving, surface-feeding fish, marine reptile breathing zones
- **Photic Zone (50-200m)**: Primary hunting grounds for plesiosaurs, rich in ammonites and belemnites
- **Bathyal Zone (200-1000m)**: Domain of massive mosasaurs and deep-water specialists
- **Abyssal Zone (1000m+)**: Mysterious depths with unknown prehistoric deep-sea creatures

### Water Body Types
- **Inland Sea**: Central massive water body connecting all coastal regions
- **Glacial Lakes**: Cold, clear mountain lakes with unique thermal properties
- **Crater Lakes**: Deep volcanic lakes with mineral-rich waters
- **Wetland Complexes**: Shallow, nutrient-rich areas supporting diverse ecosystems
- **River Deltas**: Meeting points of freshwater and marine environments

### Dynamic Systems
- **Tidal Mechanics**: Realistic tide cycles affecting coastal exploration and resource access
- **Water Temperature Zones**: Thermal layers affecting marine life distribution
- **Current Systems**: Underwater currents affecting navigation and marine animal behavior
- **Seasonal Variations**: Water level changes, ice formation, and migration patterns

### Gameplay Integration
- **Diving Mechanics**: Limited underwater exploration with breath management
- **Marine Predator AI**: Territorial behaviors and hunting patterns for aquatic dinosaurs
- **Resource Distribution**: Unique materials and fossils found only in specific water biomes
- **Navigation Challenges**: Underwater obstacles, visibility limitations, and predator avoidance

This underwater system creates a complete three-dimensional marine environment that enhances the survival experience with realistic aquatic ecosystems and challenges.
