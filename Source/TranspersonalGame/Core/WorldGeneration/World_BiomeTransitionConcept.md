# Biome Transition Zones Concept - Cretaceous Period

## Visual Concept Description
**Cretaceous Biome Transition Zones**
- Gradual transition from lush tropical rainforest to arid desert
- Prehistoric ferns and cycads in humid foreground areas
- Drought-resistant conifers and primitive flowering plants in transition
- Atmospheric perspective showing humidity level changes
- Realistic geological formations supporting different vegetation
- Natural color palette reflecting moisture gradients
- Detailed vegetation transition showing ecological adaptation

## Biome Transition Types
1. **Forest-to-Desert** - Gradual moisture reduction, vegetation adaptation
2. **Coastal-to-Inland** - Salt tolerance to freshwater species
3. **Lowland-to-Mountain** - Temperature and pressure gradients
4. **Volcanic-to-Temperate** - Mineral-rich soil transitions
5. **Wetland-to-Grassland** - Water table level changes

## Ecological Features
- Species adaptation zones showing evolutionary pressure
- Soil composition changes affecting plant growth
- Microclimate variations within transition areas
- Water source distribution influencing vegetation patterns
- Geological substrate changes supporting different ecosystems

## Technical Implementation
- Gradient-based biome blending system
- Procedural vegetation density transitions
- Soil type mapping for realistic plant distribution
- Climate zone overlap areas with mixed species
- Performance-optimized LOD for transition complexity
- Dynamic weather effects varying by biome proximity

## Gameplay Impact
- Different survival challenges in transition zones
- Unique resource availability in boundary areas
- Dinosaur migration patterns following biome edges
- Strategic locations for player settlements
- Environmental storytelling through landscape changes
