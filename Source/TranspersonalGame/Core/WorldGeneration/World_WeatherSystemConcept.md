# Dynamic Weather System Concept - Cretaceous Period

## Visual Concept Description
**Prehistoric Atmospheric Weather System**
- Dynamic storm clouds rolling over primeval landscape
- Dense fern forests swaying in prehistoric winds
- Volcanic peaks in distance creating thermal updrafts
- Dramatic lighting with rain shafts and volumetric mist
- Atmospheric pressure zones visible as subtle color gradients
- Wind patterns affecting vegetation realistically
- Cinematic prehistoric environment lighting

## Weather Zone Types
1. **TropicalStormZone** - Heavy rainfall, lightning, strong winds
2. **MistyForestZone** - Dense fog, high humidity, filtered sunlight
3. **DesertHeatZone** - Heat shimmer, dust devils, intense sun
4. **MountainCloudZone** - Cloud formation, temperature gradients
5. **CoastalWindZone** - Ocean breezes, salt spray effects

## Atmospheric Effects
- Dynamic day/night cycle affecting weather patterns
- Seasonal transitions changing weather intensity
- Atmospheric pressure zones influencing dinosaur behavior
- Wind direction systems affecting particle effects
- Volumetric fog and cloud rendering
- Realistic precipitation and storm systems

## Technical Implementation
- Weather zones as actor-based trigger volumes
- Particle systems for rain, fog, dust effects
- Dynamic lighting responding to weather conditions
- Audio zones synchronized with weather patterns
- Performance optimization through LOD systems
