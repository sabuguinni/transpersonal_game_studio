# Cretaceous Plains Biome - Visual Concept Documentation

## Overview
Expansive grassland biome representing the open plains and coastal lowlands of the Cretaceous period, characterized by vast horizons, migrating herds, and dramatic weather systems.

## Visual Characteristics

### Landscape Features
- **Terrain**: Rolling hills and flat expanses with gentle elevation changes
- **Grasslands**: Dense prehistoric grasses, sedges, and early flowering plants
- **Scattered Trees**: Isolated cycads, palm-like trees, and conifer groves
- **Rock Formations**: Weathered sandstone outcrops and ancient riverbeds

### Vegetation Patterns
- **Ground Cover**: Primitive grasses (Poaceae ancestors), ferns, and mosses
- **Shrub Layer**: Low-growing cycads, primitive flowering bushes
- **Tree Islands**: Clustered groves of conifers and early palms
- **Seasonal Variation**: Green growth in wet seasons, golden dormancy in dry periods

### Atmospheric Conditions
- **Sky Dome**: Expansive views with dramatic cloud formations
- **Lighting**: Dynamic weather lighting from golden hour to storm conditions
- **Visibility**: Long sight lines with heat shimmer effects
- **Wind Effects**: Grass waves, dust devils, and particle systems

### Water Features
- **River Systems**: Meandering waterways with oxbow lakes and wetlands
- **Seasonal Pools**: Temporary water bodies during rainy seasons
- **Mudflats**: Rich feeding areas for dinosaurs and early birds
- **Salt Deposits**: Mineral-rich areas from ancient sea beds

### Dinosaur Integration
- **Herd Behavior**: Large groups of herbivores creating worn migration paths
- **Predator Territories**: Elevated hunting grounds and ambush points
- **Nesting Grounds**: Open areas with communal nesting sites
- **Feeding Areas**: Trampled vegetation and scattered dung deposits

## Audio Environment
- **Wind Sounds**: Constant grass rustling and wind whistling
- **Wildlife**: Distant dinosaur calls, insect choruses, bird songs
- **Weather**: Thunder rolling across plains, rain on grass
- **Geological**: Occasional volcanic rumbles from distant mountains

## Gameplay Elements
- **Long-Range Visibility**: Spot predators and prey from great distances
- **Weather Exposure**: Vulnerability to storms and temperature extremes
- **Resource Scarcity**: Limited shelter and fresh water sources
- **Migration Routes**: Seasonal dinosaur movements affecting resource availability

### Strategic Considerations
- **Cover**: Sparse shelter requiring careful positioning and timing
- **Navigation**: Landmark-based wayfinding with compass mechanics
- **Hunting**: Open-ground tactics for large game
- **Base Building**: Elevated positions for defense and observation

## Environmental Hazards
- **Stampedes**: Massive herd movements that can crush unwary players
- **Flash Floods**: Rapid water level changes in river systems
- **Dust Storms**: Reduced visibility and respiratory challenges
- **Predator Ambushes**: Large carnivores using terrain features

## Performance Optimization
- **Grass System**: Procedural grass with wind animation and LOD
- **Horizon Culling**: Aggressive distance-based detail reduction
- **Weather Systems**: Scalable particle effects for different hardware
- **Herd Simulation**: LOD-based crowd rendering for distant animals

This biome provides the primary hunting and migration gameplay, emphasizing survival tactics in open environments with limited cover and abundant wildlife.
