# Cretaceous Period Atmospheric Lighting Concept

## Visual Reference Description

### Primary Lighting Characteristics
**Golden Hour Dominance**: The Cretaceous period lighting system emphasizes warm, golden sunlight that creates a rich, prehistoric atmosphere. The sun appears larger and more intense due to different atmospheric conditions 100 million years ago.

**Atmospheric Composition**: Higher CO2 levels create a thicker atmosphere with enhanced light scattering, producing:
- Deeper amber and orange tones during dawn/dusk
- More pronounced volumetric light rays through forest canopies
- Enhanced atmospheric haze that softens distant terrain
- Warmer color temperature overall (4500K-5500K range)

### Time-of-Day Progression
**Dawn (5:00-8:00)**: Deep amber light with heavy atmospheric fog, creating mysterious silhouettes of dinosaurs and prehistoric vegetation. Volumetric god rays pierce through morning mist.

**Midday (12:00-16:00)**: Bright golden sunlight with reduced atmospheric haze. Clear visibility for survival gameplay while maintaining the warm Cretaceous color palette.

**Dusk (19:00-22:00)**: Rich orange and red tones with enhanced bloom effects. Long shadows create dramatic lighting for predator encounters.

**Night (22:00-5:00)**: Cool blue moonlight with minimal visibility, emphasizing survival tension and the need for fire-based light sources.

### Volumetric Effects
- **God Rays**: Strong directional light beams through forest canopies
- **Atmospheric Scattering**: Visible light scattering in humid, dense air
- **Heat Shimmer**: Subtle distortion effects during hot midday periods
- **Fog Layers**: Ground-level mist in valleys and near water sources

### Technical Implementation
- **Lumen Global Illumination**: Dynamic bounce lighting for realistic prehistoric environments
- **Volumetric Fog**: Enhanced atmospheric depth and mood
- **Temperature-based Color Grading**: Automatic color adjustment based on time of day
- **Bloom Enhancement**: Increased bloom intensity for dramatic lighting effects

This lighting system creates an immersive prehistoric world that feels both beautiful and dangerous, supporting the survival gameplay while maintaining visual authenticity to the Cretaceous period.
