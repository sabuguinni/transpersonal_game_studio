## LORE CONTEXT: WHY IS THE HERD PANICKING?

The valley known as the Crossing is a seasonal migration corridor for a herd of approximately 400 Pachycephalosaurus and 80 Triceratops. Every dry season, the herd moves north following the river toward higher grazing ground.

**The Breaker** is a solitary Carnotaurus with a distinctive scarred flank — the result of a hunting spear wound three seasons prior. Unlike most Carnotaurus which hunt in brief ambush bursts, the Breaker has developed an unusual behaviour: sustained harassment. It does not kill immediately. It drives prey into terrain obstacles, exhausts them, then kills at its leisure. This behaviour, observed over three seasons by the tribe of Elder Kael, has earned it a name and a mythology.

The Breaker triggered the current stampede by driving the herd's rear flank for 48 hours, pushing them south into the valley instead of north along the river. The herd is panicked, disoriented, and moving in an uncontrolled mass.

**The player arrives at the valley floor at the exact moment the herd enters the narrow corridor.**

## ELDER KAEL — CHARACTER BIBLE

**Role:** Survivor NPC, quest giver, lore anchor for Act 1
**Age:** Late 50s (ancient by prehistoric standards)
**Tribe:** The Ridge People — a small band of 12-15 survivors who live on the high ground above the valley
**Personality:** Pragmatic, unsentimental, precise. Does not waste words. Has survived by being correct more often than wrong.
**Motivation:** Kael lost his brother to the valley three seasons ago. He watches the valley from the ridge. He warns strangers. Not out of kindness — out of the belief that more survivors means a stronger tribe.
**Relationship to player:** Neutral → Ally. Starts as a stranger who gives critical survival information. Becomes a quest giver for Act 1 missions. Never becomes a "friend" — becomes a respected peer.

**Physical description:**
- Tall, lean, weathered skin
- Left hand missing two fingers (old hunting injury)
- Wears a necklace of three-claw marks carved in bone — a warning talisman
- Carries a long spear with a flint head, fire-hardened shaft

### NODE 1: THE BREAKER

**[KAEL]:** "The scar-flanked beast. We call it the Breaker."

Three days along the river. He gave me directions and a warning: watch for the three-claw mark. If I see it on a tree — fresh, deep, bleeding sap — I run."*
