# T-Rex Combat AI Concept

## Visual Design
Fierce Tyrannosaurus Rex in aggressive combat stance, prehistoric jungle environment with dramatic lighting. The T-Rex is roaring with mouth open showing massive teeth, muscular body tensed for attack, detailed scales and scars from previous battles. Misty atmospheric background with ancient ferns creates an intimidating prehistoric setting.

## Combat Characteristics
- **Health**: 200 HP (highest among predators)
- **Attack Damage**: 50 (devastating bite attacks)
- **Attack Range**: 300 units (long reach with massive head)
- **Movement Speed**: 400 units/sec (surprisingly fast for size)
- **Detection Radius**: 1500 units (excellent vision and smell)
- **Aggression Level**: 0.9 (extremely aggressive apex predator)
- **Attack Cooldown**: 3.0 seconds (powerful but slower attacks)

## Behavioral States
1. **Idle**: Scanning environment for threats/prey
2. **Patrolling**: Territorial movement patterns
3. **Hunting**: Active pursuit of detected targets
4. **Attacking**: Devastating bite and claw attacks
5. **Defending**: Protecting territory or young
6. **Fleeing**: Rare retreat when severely wounded

## Combat Tactics
- Uses intimidation roars to frighten smaller creatures
- Charges at targets with devastating momentum
- Utilizes powerful bite as primary weapon
- Tail swipe for area damage to multiple enemies
- Territorial behavior - defends specific areas aggressively

## Audio Cues
- Deep, thunderous roars for intimidation
- Heavy footstep impacts that can be heard from distance
- Aggressive snarling during combat
- Victory roars after successful hunts

This concept serves as the foundation for implementing the most feared predator in the prehistoric world.
