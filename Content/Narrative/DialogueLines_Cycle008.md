## Narrative Bible Notes

### Core Tension
The dinosaur migration southward is not random — a volcanic event in the northern highlands is displacing apex predators into previously safe tribal territory. The player must survive escalating predator pressure while gathering enough resources to relocate the tribe.

### Character Voice Profiles
- **ElderHunter**: Slow, measured, observational. Has seen much. Speaks in patterns and seasons.
- **ScoutWarrior**: Clipped, tactical, urgent. Every word serves survival. No philosophy.
- **TribalLeader**: Authoritative but burdened. Weighs lives against decisions.
- **Gatherer**: Practical, worried, maternal/paternal. Thinks in terms of food and children.

### Dialogue Design Rules
1. No character explains the world — they react to it
2. Every line implies danger, scarcity, or urgency
3. Primitive vocabulary — no abstract concepts
4. Information is delivered through personal experience, not exposition
5. Player agency: NPCs ask, player decides
