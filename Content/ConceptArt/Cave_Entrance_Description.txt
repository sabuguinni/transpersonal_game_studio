CAVE ENTRANCE SYSTEM - CONCEPT ART DESCRIPTION
==============================================

ENVIRONMENT: Prehistoric Cave Entrance
LOCATION: Rocky biome area (5000, -3000, 200)

VISUAL ELEMENTS:
- Massive natural rock arch (8x6x5 scale) as main entrance
- 6 stalactites hanging from arch ceiling (0.4x0.4x1.5-3.5 scale)
- 12 large boulders forming perimeter (2-5 scale, 3-7 height variation)
- Moss and lichen covering rock surfaces
- Dark interior receding into mystery
- Dappled sunlight creating dramatic shadows

GEOLOGICAL FEATURES:
- Weathered limestone/sandstone formations
- Natural erosion patterns on rock surfaces
- Stratified rock layers visible in arch
- Fallen debris at entrance base
- Cracks and fissures in rock face

LIGHTING:
- Strong directional sunlight from above
- Deep shadows in cave interior
- Volumetric light shafts through gaps
- Ambient occlusion in crevices
- Cool blue-grey tones in shadows

ATMOSPHERE:
- Mysterious and inviting
- Sense of depth and exploration
- Ancient, untouched environment
- Slightly dangerous (potential for collapse)

VEGETATION:
- Moss patches on north-facing rocks
- Small ferns growing in cracks
- Vines hanging from arch edges
- Lichen covering weathered surfaces

GAMEPLAY IMPLICATIONS:
- Shelter from weather/predators
- Resource gathering (minerals, mushrooms)
- Dinosaur den potential (territorial danger)
- Navigation landmark
- Quest location (exploration objective)

- Stalactites use procedural drip patterns
- Moss/lichen use vertex painting for variation
