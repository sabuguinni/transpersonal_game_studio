VOLCANIC BIOME - CONCEPT ART DESCRIPTION
========================================

ENVIRONMENT: Prehistoric Volcanic Landscape
LOCATION: Southwest region of MinPlayableMap (-6000, -5000, 300)

VISUAL ELEMENTS:
- Active volcanic cone (12x12x15 scale) as central landmark
- 4 lava pools with glowing red-orange material (scales 3-6)
- 30 scattered volcanic rocks (dark basalt, sharp edges, 1.5-5 scale)
- 15 charred dead trees (blackened trunks, no foliage, 3-6m tall)
- Ash-covered terrain with cracks showing red glow beneath
- Smoke/steam rising from fissures and lava pools

LIGHTING:
- Dramatic red-orange ambient from lava pools
- High contrast between bright lava and dark rocks
- Volumetric fog/smoke for atmospheric depth
- Rim lighting on rocks from lava glow

ATMOSPHERE:
- Desolate and dangerous
- Heat haze distortion over lava
- Ash particles floating in air
- Oppressive, hostile environment

GAMEPLAY IMPLICATIONS:
- High-risk exploration area
- Heat damage zones near lava
- Unique volcanic resources (obsidian, sulfur)
- Potential dinosaur nesting grounds (heat-loving species)
- Natural barrier/landmark for navigation

TECHNICAL NOTES:
- Lava pools use emissive materials (red-orange, high intensity)
- Volcanic rocks use dark basalt PBR materials
- Charred trees use blackened wood materials
- Particle systems for smoke/ash/heat distortion
- Post-process volume for color grading (warm tones)

ACTORS SPAWNED: 50 total (1 cone + 4 lava pools + 30 rocks + 15 trees)
