# Atmospheric Lighting Concepts - Visual Reference

## Forest Interior Lighting

### Dappled Light System
**Concept**: Sunlight filtering through dense prehistoric fern canopy
- **Light Pattern**: Irregular patches of bright illumination
- **Movement**: Subtle animation as canopy sways
- **Color Temperature**: Warm golden (3200K) in light patches
- **Shadow Areas**: Cool blue-green ambient (6500K)
- **Contrast Ratio**: 8:1 between light and shadow areas

### Volumetric Atmosphere
**Dust and Particle Effects**:
- **Floating Spores**: Prehistoric plant matter in air
- **Dust Motes**: Illuminated by light shafts
- **Humidity Haze**: Subtle atmospheric scattering
- **Insect Swarms**: Occasional flying particles in light beams

### Material Interactions
**Surface Responses**:
- **Moss**: Subsurface scattering for organic glow
- **Wet Leaves**: Specular highlights and reflections
- **Tree Bark**: Rough surface with deep shadow crevices
- **Fallen Logs**: Rim lighting to separate from background

## Cave and Cavern Lighting

### Natural Light Sources
**Primary Illumination**:
- **Cave Mouth**: Bright exterior light creating silhouette
- **Light Wells**: Openings in cave ceiling
- **Reflected Light**: Bounced illumination from rock surfaces
- **Bioluminescence**: Glowing fungi and prehistoric organisms

### Atmospheric Effects
**Cave Environment**:
- **Cool Temperature**: 4000-5000K color temperature
- **High Humidity**: Visible moisture in air
- **Echo Lighting**: Multiple bounce reflections
- **Depth Cueing**: Atmospheric perspective in deep caves

### Mystery and Exploration
**Gameplay Lighting**:
- **Torch Light**: Player-carried dynamic light source
- **Hidden Areas**: Subtle illumination hints at secrets
- **Danger Zones**: Ominous lighting for predator lairs
- **Discovery Moments**: Dramatic reveals with lighting changes

## Savanna and Plains Lighting

### Open Sky Illumination
**Vast Landscape Lighting**:
- **Infinite Sky Dome**: Realistic atmospheric scattering
- **Horizon Glow**: Enhanced luminance at horizon line
- **Cloud Shadows**: Dynamic shadow patterns on terrain
- **Heat Shimmer**: Atmospheric distortion effects

### Seasonal Variations
**Time-Based Changes**:
- **Dry Season**: Dusty atmosphere, warm color palette
- **Wet Season**: Clear air, enhanced contrast
- **Storm Approach**: Dramatic cloud lighting
- **Post-Rain**: Enhanced saturation and reflections

## Swamp and Wetland Lighting

### Mysterious Atmosphere
**Moody Environment**:
- **Filtered Sunlight**: Heavy atmospheric scattering
- **Water Reflections**: Mirror-like surface reflections
- **Mist and Fog**: Ground-level atmospheric effects
- **Bioluminescent Accents**: Glowing swamp life

### Danger Indicators
**Visual Storytelling**:
- **Murky Depths**: Dark underwater areas
- **Predator Eyes**: Subtle glowing points in darkness
- **Poisonous Plants**: Unnatural color illumination
- **Unstable Ground**: Visual cues through lighting

## Technical Implementation Notes

### Performance Optimization
- **LOD System**: Distance-based lighting complexity
- **Culling**: Frustum and occlusion culling for lights
- **Temporal Upsampling**: Smooth volumetric effects
- **Dynamic Resolution**: Adaptive quality based on performance

### Artistic Direction
- **Color Harmony**: Complementary warm/cool relationships
- **Contrast Management**: Balanced exposure across scenes
- **Mood Consistency**: Lighting supports narrative tone
- **Player Guidance**: Subtle lighting cues for navigation

This atmospheric lighting system creates immersive prehistoric environments that enhance both the visual spectacle and gameplay experience, guiding players through a world that feels both beautiful and dangerous.
