### Lighting Philosophy (Roger Deakins Principle)
> "The player doesn't notice correct lighting — they only notice wrong lighting."

The Cretaceous world should feel:
- **Dawn/Morning**: Cool blue-purple shadows, warm amber highlights, heavy ground fog
- **Midday**: Harsh overhead light, deep shadows under canopy, heat haze
- **Dusk**: Long warm shadows, orange-red sky, silhouetted dinosaurs
- **Night**: Moonlit blue, bioluminescent patches, deep darkness with danger

This cycle establishes the **midday baseline** — the foundation all other time-of-day states build from.
