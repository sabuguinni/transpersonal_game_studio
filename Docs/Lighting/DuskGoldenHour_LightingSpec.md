## Overview

The **Dusk / Golden Hour** palette is the most cinematically powerful lighting state in the game.
It occurs approximately 30 minutes before sunset, when the sun sits at a low angle (-8° pitch)
and casts long, warm shadows across the Cretaceous landscape.

This is the lighting state that makes the world feel **dangerous, beautiful, and alive**.
Predators become silhouettes. Ruins glow amber. The jungle breathes.

## Emotional Intent

> "The player crests a hill and sees the valley below bathed in golden light.
> A T-Rex silhouette moves through the amber haze. They have maybe 20 minutes
> before darkness falls. Every second of this light feels precious."

The dusk palette creates:
- **Urgency** — night is coming, predators become more dangerous
- **Beauty** — the world rewards exploration with visual payoff
- **Danger** — low sun creates long shadows where threats can hide
- **Scale** — warm light on distant terrain emphasizes the world's vastness
