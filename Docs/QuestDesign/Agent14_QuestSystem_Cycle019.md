## Quest 1: "The Migration"

**Type:** Escort / Combat  
**Quest Giver:** `Tribe_Human_Elder` at (-500, -800)  
**Trigger Actor:** `QuestTrigger_Migration_Start` — TriggerSphere radius 3x at (-500, -800, 120)

### Objective Chain
1. **Talk to the Elder** — approach `Tribe_Human_Elder`, hear voice line (TTS generated)
2. **Find the Herd** — navigate to `QuestMarker_Migration_HerdLead` (green light at 3000, 3200)
3. **Protect the Stragglers** — 3 Brachiosaurus at rear of formation enter `QuestZone_Migration_RaptorAmbush` (TriggerBox 8x8x4 at 1800, 2200)
4. **Defeat Raptor Ambush** — `Pack_Raptor_FlankL` + `Pack_Raptor_FlankR` attack stragglers
5. **Complete Migration** — escort herd lead to destination (3600, 4000)

### Rewards
- Unlock `Tribe_Human_Hunter` as companion NPC
- Receive 3x Brachio hide (crafting material for warm clothing)
- Unlock biome: Northern Savanna

### Emotional Arc
Player starts as outsider helping tribe. Protecting the herd creates attachment to the animals. Raptor ambush forces a choice: fight or flee. Completing the migration earns tribal trust.

### Voice Lines
- **Elder (start):** "The herd moves at dawn. Follow the Brachiosaurus trail north — but beware, the raptors hunt the stragglers. Stay close to the treeline and move fast."
  - Audio: `tts/1781839189066_Tribe_Elder.mp3`

## Quest 2: "Predator Alert"

**Type:** Escort / Stealth  
**Quest Giver:** `Tribe_Human_Lookout` (automatic trigger when T-Rex enters detection zone)  
**Trigger Actor:** `QuestTrigger_PredatorAlert_Start` — TriggerSphere 5x at (200, -600, 120)

### Objective Chain
1. **T-Rex Detected** — `QuestZone_TRex_DetectionRadius` (TriggerBox 20x20x6 at 800, 400) fires when T-Rex patrol enters
2. **Lookout Alerts Tribe** — `Tribe_Human_Lookout` voice line triggers (TTS generated)
3. **Escort Children to Cave** — move `Tribe_Human_Child_001` + `Tribe_Human_Child_002` to `QuestMarker_Cave_Entrance` (orange light at -1200, -400)
4. **Avoid T-Rex** — player must not enter `QuestZone_TRex_DetectionRadius` while escorting
5. **Seal Cave Entrance** — interact with boulder near cave entrance

### Rewards
- Unlock `ScatterZone_Cave` as permanent safe zone
- Receive flint knife (crafting upgrade)
- Tribe fear stat reduced (tribe trusts player more)

### Emotional Arc
Urgency and fear. Player must prioritize the vulnerable (children) over fighting. Stealth and route planning matter more than combat skill. Success = tribe survival, failure = children captured/killed.

### Voice Lines
- **Lookout (alert):** "Hunters! The T-Rex has crossed the river. Take the children to the cave — now! I will hold the treeline with the others. Do not look back, just run!"
  - Audio: `tts/1781839205953_Tribe_Lookout.mp3`

## Quest 3: "First Hunt"

**Type:** Crafting + Combat (Tutorial Quest)  
**Quest Giver:** `Tribe_Human_Hunter` (unlocked after Quest 1)  
**Trigger Actor:** `QuestTrigger_FirstHunt_CraftingArea` — TriggerSphere 2.5x at (-300, -1000, 120)

### Objective Chain
1. **Gather Materials** — collect 2x `Resource_Rock` + 1x `Resource_Stick` from nearby pickups
2. **Craft Stone Spear** — interact with crafting area trigger, combine materials
3. **Track Raptor Scout** — follow tracks from camp to `QuestTarget_RaptorScout_Hunt` (red light at 600, 900)
4. **Hunt the Scout** — kill isolated `Pack_Raptor_ScoutB` (weakest raptor, alone)
5. **Return with Proof** — bring raptor claw back to Hunter NPC

### Rewards
- Stone Spear (permanent weapon unlock)
- Hunting skill +1 (increases damage vs small predators)
- Unlock crafting recipes: Bone Knife, Leather Wrap

### Emotional Arc
First kill is significant. The raptor is alone and scared — player feels the weight of taking a life. Hunter NPC acknowledges this: "You did what was necessary. The tribe eats tonight."

### Design Notes
- This is the crafting tutorial — teaches resource gathering + crafting loop
- Raptor scout is isolated from pack (safe to fight alone, 1v1)
- Tracking mechanic: follow footprints (decal actors) from camp to hunt zone
- Connects to CraftingSystem.cpp (Agent #14 previous cycle)
