## Quest Design Notes (Pawel Sasko / Jonathan Blow lens)
- **"First Tools" quest arc**: player gathers rock+rock+stick → crafts axe at the station → the mechanic itself is the statement: survival requires deliberate resource choices, not abundance. The axe isn't a reward, it's proof the player understood the world's scarcity.
- **"The Restless Herd" hunt/track quest**: built on the tagged `Quest_HuntTarget` actor. Emotional arc: the player isn't hunting for XP, they're hunting because the camp's meat stores are gone. Failure state (spooking the animal) has real consequence — it migrates further, extending the quest rather than failing it outright.
