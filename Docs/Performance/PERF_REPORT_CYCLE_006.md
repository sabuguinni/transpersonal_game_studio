## Rendering Architecture Notes

### Lumen (Global Illumination + Reflections)
- Both enabled — required for prehistoric world visual quality
- Lumen is hardware-accelerated on RTX cards; software fallback on older GPUs
- Console target (30fps) may require `r.Lumen.GlobalIllumination.Allow 0` with baked lighting

### Shadow Budget
- `r.Shadow.MaxResolution 1024` — adequate for character/dino shadows at play distances
- `r.Shadow.DistanceScale 0.6` — shadows fade at ~60% of default distance
- For console: reduce to `r.Shadow.MaxResolution 512` + `r.Shadow.DistanceScale 0.4`

### LOD Strategy
- Static meshes: LOD0 at ≤1200 units, LOD1 at ≤3000, LOD2 at ≤6000
- Foliage: LOD transitions at 80% of default distances
- Dinosaur skeletal meshes: author LOD1 (50% tris) and LOD2 (20% tris) for distance

### View Distance
- `r.ViewDistanceScale 0.85` — 15% reduction from default
- Prehistoric world benefits from atmospheric fog to hide pop-in at reduced view distance
- Agent #08 (Lighting) should ensure ExponentialHeightFog is configured to mask LOD transitions
